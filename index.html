System Administration:
Tech teams may include system administrators who are responsible for configuring, monitoring, and maintaining the organization's servers, networks, and IT infrastructure. This includes tasks such as managing user accounts, ensuring system security, and troubleshooting technical issues.

Database Management:
Database administrators within the tech team manage and maintain the organization's databases. This includes tasks such as designing database structures, optimizing database performance, and ensuring data integrity and security.

IT Support:
Tech teams often provide IT support to end-users within the organization. This includes troubleshooting technical issues, providing assistance with software and hardware problems, and responding to helpdesk tickets and inquiries.
